What is this?

This is a browser-based tool that uses Web Bluetooth to connect to the Specialized Globe Haul ST / LT e-bike in order to enable or disable the optional throttle and switch display units between imperial and metric

What do I need?

  1. A Globe Haul ST or LT bike
  2. A desktop or mobile browser that supports Web Bluetooth (Bluefy on iOS)

How do I use it?

  1. Power on your Globe Haul
  2. Push Connect to Globe Haul
  3. Select your bike from the list
    • EH02_42747 or similar
  4. Push
    • Enable Throttle or Disable Throttle
    • Imperial or Metric
  5. Done, you can now
    • Click Disconnect
    • Power off the bike
    • Close the browser

Privacy

This tool runs entirely in your browser and respects your privacy. No data is collected, stored, or sent anywhere. There are no cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind
